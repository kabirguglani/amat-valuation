## Overview

Full equity valuation of **Applied Materials Inc. (NASDAQ: AMAT)** — the world's leading supplier of semiconductor manufacturing equipment — conducted as part of the **CFA Research Challenge** at Temple University.

Applied Materials serves chipmakers across memory, foundry, logic, and display segments with operations in 24 countries. China represented 27.3% of FY2023 revenue, making geopolitical risk a central valuation consideration.

## Investment Thesis

**Why HOLD (Short Term):**
- Near-term China regulatory headwinds limit upside
- Semiconductor capex growth not expected to accelerate in short term
- Stock trading close to fair value at $197 vs DCF of $189

**Why Bullish (Long Term):**
- Structural AI/data centre demand drives sustained semiconductor capex
- AMAT's Semiconductor Systems segment growing from 63.6% → 74.9% of revenue (2018–2023)
- Strong moat: low threat of entry, low threat of substitutes (Porter's 5 Forces)
- NetZero 2040 commitment and ESG score 69/100 — increasingly important for institutional investors
